Time is Life!
We are following the current scene of medicine tragedies around the world.
One of these is babies born too soon, before 37 weeks of pregnancy, to say premature birth.
World Health Organization reported that every year about 15 million babies are born prematurely.

Early birth is largest single cause of death or long term disability.
Imagine 1 million die yearly out of preterm.

More than 75% of these babies can be saved though providing right medical care in time and increasing this number by delivering healthy pregnancy consciousness.
Hence, one of the biggest challenges is to provide a suitable infant incubator in the right time to them.

We are aiming at providing fast and effective technological solution through publishing smart web and mobile application that gives data about current available near incubators.
